Help

This calculator supports basic arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Voice Commands

To perform multiplication, you can say a command like:

"Multiply 3 and 2"

To perform Addition, you can say a command like:

"Add 3 and 2"

To perform Division, you can say a command like:

"Divide 3 and 2"

To perform Subtraction, you can say a command like:

"Subtract 3 and 2"

The calculator will calculate the result and display it on the screen.
